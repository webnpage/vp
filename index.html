Iron Man

Directed by Jon Favreau, "Iron Man" is the film that kicked off the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Released in 2008, the movie stars Robert Downey Jr. as Tony Stark, a brilliant and wealthy industrialist who finds himself at the center of a life-altering experience. The story begins with Tony Stark, a genius inventor, and CEO of Stark Industries, being captured by terrorists while demonstrating his company's latest weapon in Afghanistan. Forced to build a weapon of mass destruction, Stark instead creates a powerful suit of armor to escape. This event marks a turning point for Stark, leading him to reevaluate his life and the impact of his company's weapons on the world.
